My Best Friend, Mogli

I have the best dog in the universe. That's a pretty tall order; but I guarantee I'm telling the truth.

I love and adore him for many reasons. I'll list a few below:

  • He is a cuddle-bug.
  • He loves to go for walks.
  • He's vocal when he wants to be

Mogli is also incredibly intelligent. He knows the following:

  1. Sit
  2. Stay
  3. Drink water
  4. Are you hungry?
  5. Do you want to go for a walk?

I'm sure some of you are cat people, or you just don't see what's so great about Mogli.

I have one last point to make.

JUST LOOK AT HIM!
